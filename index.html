Yes. We're back.



On the 21st of March 2015, we showed the world that Malaysian bronies mean business. What was supposed to be a one-off party for just a handful of us graduated into memories that will last a lifetime. We don't want it to end there, and we're sure you feel the same way too.



Create in Harmony.



TWENTY SIXTEEN - THE YEAR OF THE ARTIST



The universe of FiM is made much more beautiful by the tireless work of artists - musicians, painters, drawers, animators, writers and many more. The multitude of work forged with passion is an everlasting testament to the beauty of this fandom. The Friendship Express honors these creations and the people who make them possible with our theme for 2016 -

Create in Harmony.



Meet Rosa



In the spirit of Friendship that we are all rallied upon, allow us to introduce to you a new friend - Rosa Blossomheart!


Rosa is our resident therapist, advice mare as well as the official mascot of The Friendship Express 2016. She embodies the spirit of art and creativity and how it can help everyone express themselves where words simply will not suffice.




Check out Rosa's favourite hangout at TFE's Tumblr.
